Why It May Fit

  • It is a serious payments fintech close to home.

  • This is less obviously designed for someone still clarifying direction.

Recommended Next Step

  • Re-check later for entry-level, junior, or graduate-style openings in Amsterdam.
  • Use it mainly as evidence that fintech can mean payments and operations, not only trading or coding.
