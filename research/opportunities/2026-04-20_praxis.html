Summary

Praxis is real and philosophically aligned with this project, but it is less obviously accessible than Claude suggested. The official site positions it for young adults who want to build skills and avoid college, with 1-on-1 mentorship and peer community. The public page also says it can be completed online from anywhere in the United States, which may be a practical constraint from the Netherlands.

Why It May Fit

  • It takes the anti-default-path instinct seriously without becoming vague.
  • It offers mentorship and peer structure, which a young adult may need.
  • It focuses on practical career development rather than academic status.

Risks / Constraints

  • The official site points to US-based participation, which may limit fit from Europe.
  • It is not specifically fintech or sailing.
  • It could become more ideological than practical if not grounded in real output.

Recommended Next Step

  • Keep it as a nontraditional structure option, but not a top current path.
  • Verify whether non-US participants are realistically supported.
  • Use it more as a model of structured independence than as a presumed direct option.
