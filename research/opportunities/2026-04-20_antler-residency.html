Antler Residency

Antler is a full-time founder residency designed for ambitious startup builders. The upside is clear: strong peers, intensity, investor access, and a high-performance environment. The risk is also clear: it is for people who are ready to go all-in, not for someone who wants to "explore" loosely.

Antler Residency

  • Category: gap year
  • Organization: Antler
  • Location: Multiple cities including US, UK, and Europe
  • Language: English
  • Format: Full-time, in-person founder residency
  • Best for: Someone who is serious about going all-in on building a startup and wants a high-ambition founder environment

Summary

Antler is a full-time founder residency designed for ambitious startup builders. The upside is clear: strong peers, intensity, investor access, and a high-performance environment. The risk is also clear: it is for people who are ready to go all-in, not for someone who wants to "explore" loosely.

Why It May Fit

  • It directly targets the desire for a more ambitious environment.
  • It is international and English-speaking.
  • It fits the participant's pull toward AI, finance, and startup building more than conventional university life.
  • It could test whether the participant wants to be around founder-level intensity or just likes the idea of it.

Risks / Constraints

  • Antler says it is highly selective.
  • It expects full-time commitment and explicitly says it does not work alongside a full-time job, university degree, or side commitments.
  • Visa eligibility matters and Antler advises applicants to make sure they can legally enter and stay in the location they choose.
  • This is better for someone ready to build now, not someone still very unclear and avoiding foundational work.

Recommended Next Step

  • Keep this as a stretch option, not the default plan.
  • If the participant starts building seriously over the next 6 to 12 weeks, revisit Antler as a next-stage environment.
  • If not, treat it as a signal of the level of seriousness the participant would need before applying.
