Entrepreneurs First Bridge Residency

  • Category: founder residency
  • Organization: Entrepreneurs First
  • Location: San Francisco, California, United States
  • Language: English
  • Format: 8-week full-time, in-person residency for young Europeans
  • Best for: Someone who is unusually ambitious, wants a founder-caliber peer group, and is ready to spend a summer building with full commitment
  • Timing / deadline: Summer 2026 cohort starts in August 2026; final application deadline listed as 1 June 2026

Summary

Entrepreneurs First's Bridge Residency is one of the clearest high-ambition founder options currently visible for a young European. It is not a casual exploration program. EF says it is full-time, in person, and aimed at outlier early-career talent who can find a cofounder and start building fast.

Why It May Fit

  • It directly addresses the stronger-peers problem with a high-performance founder environment.
  • It is explicitly designed for young Europeans and is fully English-speaking.
  • the participant does not need a cofounder or finished startup idea before applying.
  • It could reveal whether the participant wants real founder pressure or mainly likes the image of it.

Risks / Constraints

  • EF says the program is full-time and in person, and expects relocation to a hub city.
  • It is selective and aimed at people who have already shown unusual outcomes relative to peers.
  • Visa support is limited; EF says it does not provide visas, though it may help with applications in some cases.
  • This is a poor fit if the participant still needs a gentler proving ground before entering elite founder rooms.

Recommended Next Step

  • Keep this as a stretch target for summer 2026 rather than the default plan.
  • If the participant can build a credible project or portfolio over the next 4 to 6 weeks, prepare a draft application before 1 June 2026.
