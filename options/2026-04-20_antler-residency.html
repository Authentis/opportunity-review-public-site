Full-time, in-person founder residency. Multiple cities including US, UK, and Europe

Why It May Be Interesting

  • It directly targets the desire for a more ambitious environment.
  • It is international and English-speaking.
  • It fits your pull toward AI, finance, and startup building more than conventional university life.
  • It could test whether you want to be around founder-level intensity or just likes the idea of it.

Things To Check

  • Antler says it is highly selective.
  • It expects full-time commitment and explicitly says it does not work alongside a full-time job, university degree, or side commitments.
  • Visa eligibility matters and Antler advises applicants to make sure they can legally enter and stay in the location they choose.
  • This is better for someone ready to build now, not someone still very unclear and avoiding foundational work.
