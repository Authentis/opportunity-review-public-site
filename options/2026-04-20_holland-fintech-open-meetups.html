Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is geographically easy from the Netherlands.
  • It is directly related to fintech without demanding a full commitment first.
  • It can test the “better environment” question quickly.
  • It may help separate real interest in fintech from vague status attraction.

Things To Check

  • This is a network and event layer, not a full developmental path.
  • Membership is mainly company-oriented, so not every offering is naturally designed for an individual 20-year-old.
  • Open meetups may be useful, but only if you are willing to show up, speak, and keep going.
