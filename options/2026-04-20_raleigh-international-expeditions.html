Why It May Be Interesting

  • It offers a different environment with clear adult structure.
  • It makes contribution and endurance visible rather than abstract.
  • It gives a concrete way to experience a different peer environment with real tasks.
  • It has more peer and staff scaffolding than a less structured gap-year option.

Things To Check

  • It is expensive and may require fundraising.
  • It is not fintech or career-specific.
  • The expedition model may be more useful as a reset than as a direct path.
  • If you are drawn more to status than contribution, this may lose its appeal once the reality sets in.
