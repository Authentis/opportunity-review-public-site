Why It May Be Interesting

  • It offers staged progress and expert mentoring.
  • It is run by an EU body, which makes it more credible and less hype-driven.
  • It could test whether you can stay inside a real process over months.
  • It gives access to a broader innovation network and visible milestones.

Things To Check

  • The thematic cohorts are not directly fintech-centred.
  • It is still entrepreneurial and requires a credible innovation idea.
  • It may be better for science-based or innovation-heavy ideas than for a more exploratory phase.
