Why It May Be Interesting

  • It directly addresses the stronger-peers problem with a high-performance founder environment.
  • It is explicitly designed for young Europeans and is fully English-speaking.
  • you does not need a cofounder or finished startup idea before applying.
  • It could reveal whether you want real founder pressure or mainly likes the image of it.

Things To Check

  • EF says the program is full-time and in person, and expects relocation to a hub city.
  • It is selective and aimed at people who have already shown unusual outcomes relative to peers.
  • Visa support is limited; EF says it does not provide visas, though it may help with applications in some cases.
  • This is a poor fit if you still needs a gentler proving ground before entering elite founder rooms.
