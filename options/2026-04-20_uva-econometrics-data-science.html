Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is much closer to your stated math plus finance / AI direction than a generic economics degree.
  • Amsterdam is a more alive and international city environment than the narrower Dutch settings already discussed.
  • The programme is academically serious without being purely abstract.
  • The small-group structure may be better than a large anonymous bachelor.

Things To Check

  • It is still a demanding quantitative academic path and not a soft re-entry.
  • It assumes you can tolerate structured study, math pressure, and programming work over time.
  • Dutch university culture and local context may still be part of the picture even if the programme itself is English-taught.
  • Timing is tight for the current cycle.
