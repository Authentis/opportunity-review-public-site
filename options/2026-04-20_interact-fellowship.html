Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is one of the better peer-quality and belonging-oriented additions from Claude's pass.
  • It is more community- and reflection-shaped than coding- or résumé-shaped.
  • It offers a strong external network without requiring immediate commitment to a startup or degree.

Things To Check

  • It is not clearly open right now from the current official page.
  • The live page does not clearly spell out the full current eligibility rules.
  • The older official beta page suggests a young-technologist profile and likely under-24 positioning, but this should be treated as supporting evidence rather than the definitive live requirement set.
  • This is more selective fellowship/community than concrete employment path.
