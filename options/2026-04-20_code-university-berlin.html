Why It May Be Interesting

  • It keeps the academic branch alive without collapsing back into ordinary university.
  • Berlin is already a credible environment for you.
  • The admissions process explicitly says no technical knowledge is required and focuses on motivation, fit, and project-based learning.
  • It also includes a Business Management and Entrepreneurship bachelor's, which makes it broader than a pure coding route.

Things To Check

  • It is a private university, so cost is a real issue.
  • It still requires academic paperwork and a serious multi-year commitment.
  • If you mainly want freedom and dislike formal study structure, the project-based pedagogy may still not be enough.
