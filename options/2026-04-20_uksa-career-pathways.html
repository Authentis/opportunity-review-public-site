Why It May Be Interesting

  • It offers exactly the kind of staged competence-building that looks useful here.
  • It provides respected external adults and visible progression.
  • It makes early skill-building structured, visible, and normal.
  • It is a more serious sailing ladder than a one-off adventure format.

Things To Check

  • It is expensive.
  • It is career-specific and may be too committing if sailing is still only an early sailing idea.
  • It moves quickly toward professional maritime identity, which may be too narrow for you right now.
  • UK logistics and relocation matter.
