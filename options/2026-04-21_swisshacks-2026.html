Why It May Be Interesting

  • It turns fintech from a vague interest into a practical team test.
  • It gives you a short, intense, real deadline.
  • It includes team matching, so you do not need an existing group.
  • It gives exposure to finance, AI, digital assets, and product ideas without a long commitment.
  • It may help answer whether fintech people and problems actually energize you.
  • It creates output: a prototype, pitch, and story, not just attendance.

Things To Check

  • It is intense and public; you would need to tolerate beginner status and collaboration.
  • It is only 48 hours, so it does not solve longer-term structure by itself.
  • Zurich logistics and accommodation still matter.
  • Attending with no preparation and no willingness to talk to people would sharply reduce the value.
