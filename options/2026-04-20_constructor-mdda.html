Why It May Be Interesting

  • It combines business, entrepreneurship, and data work rather than forcing a pure business-school or pure computer-science identity.
  • The international residential model may matter if peer environment is a real part of the problem.
  • Germany is a more interesting academic geography for this project than defaulting again to the Netherlands.
  • The built-in internship and study-abroad structure reduce drift.

Things To Check

  • Cost is a real issue even with scholarships or financing.
  • It is still an academic programme, so a stronger environment would not remove the need for sustained effort.
  • The business orientation is stronger than in a pure math or econometrics route.
  • It may appeal partly for image if not pressure-tested properly.
