Why It May Be Interesting

  • It respects the math side of your identity instead of treating it as decorative.
  • It connects math, AI, and economics in one path.
  • Bocconi offers a stronger peer and city environment than many default alternatives.
  • Milan also keeps you near finance and startup ecosystems rather than away from them.

Things To Check

  • It is still a demanding academic track with real theoretical depth.
  • If your main problem is not curriculum but tolerance for imperfection and sustained effort, a better university alone will not solve that.
  • It still requires clearing a real admissions process.
  • Cost and scholarship picture should still be checked separately.
  • It is more academically rigorous than entrepreneurial in the day-to-day experience.
