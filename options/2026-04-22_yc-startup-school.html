Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is accessible from anywhere and do not require university enrollment.
  • It creates a weekly progress surface, which matters for deadline visibility.
  • It can test startup interest without making you perform a founder interest in public.
  • The curriculum is high-signal and founder-practical rather than generic motivational content.
  • It can be paired with a small project or side experiment.

Things To Check

  • It is online and therefore weak as an isolation antidote.
  • It requires self-direction; nobody will notice drift the way a residential program might.
  • It can become passive content consumption if not paired with a real project.
  • Co-founder matching may be premature unless you have a concrete direction.
