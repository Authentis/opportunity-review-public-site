Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is finance-specific and English-speaking.
  • It is more structured than applying cold into finance.
  • It combines work, study, salary, mentoring, and a cohort-like learning path.
  • It avoids the immediate UK visa / right-to-work problem if EU / Ireland work access is straightforward.
  • It could be more realistic than a graduate programme because it is built for school leavers / early entrants.

Things To Check

  • Current employer openings are cycle-based and may already be closed for 2026.
  • You would need to accept a two-year professional route, not just an exploratory event.
  • It is more operations / financial-services entry than elite quant or startup finance.
  • Ireland relocation still requires practical planning.
  • Eligibility needs careful checking against your secondary-school qualifications and nationality / right-to-work status.
