Why It May Be Interesting

  • It gives external structure and adult expectations without requiring a finished career plan.
  • It may involve writing, communication, project coordination, photos, local field work, and cross-border topics.
  • It is in-person, not remote.
  • It can build CV proof and confidence through real tasks.
  • It may suit someone interested in travel, systems, Europe, mobility, sustainability, or public projects.

Things To Check

  • It is not residential by default; accommodation may be the volunteer's responsibility, though support and stipend exist.
  • Language requirements can be strict for specific placements.
  • It may be too administrative unless the project topic is well chosen.
  • Selection is real: IVY preselects candidates and host organisations choose.
