Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is directly fintech-focused and international.
  • It could help you see whether fintech is interesting in practice when exposed to the actual ecosystem.
  • It offers a broad view of the field: startups, policy, payments, digital assets, AI, and finance infrastructure.

Things To Check

  • This is an immersion and networking option, not a structured developmental program by itself.
  • The audience may skew older, more senior, and more established.
  • Cost, tickets, and practical access are not clearly simple from the landing page.
  • It is only useful if you are willing to prepare, speak to people, and use the event actively.
  • Attending with no independent role or prepared plan would reduce the value.
