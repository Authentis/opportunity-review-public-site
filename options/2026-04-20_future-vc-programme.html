Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is a cleaner and more structured path into venture than trying to network blindly.
  • It could test whether you likes markets, founders, and investment thinking, not only startup image.
  • The program combines learning, mentoring, and real exposure rather than pure theory.
  • It is more international and better peer-signaling than generic business jobs.

Things To Check

  • Venture capital can become a way of orbiting action without building anything himself.
  • The current public timing details looked stale and need verification.
  • The strongest value may still come after you has built some clearer startup or project credibility.
  • If you are drawn mainly by status, this could become a distancing move rather than a proving move.
