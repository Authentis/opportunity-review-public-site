Why It May Be Interesting

  • It directly addresses the "better environment" problem by putting you around smart, serious programmers.
  • It is self-directed, which may fit your dislike of rigid conventional structures.
  • It is still structured enough to avoid pure drift: fixed batch dates, full-time commitment, peer expectations, and visible work.
  • It could help separate genuine interest in building from abstract complaints about school.
  • It is free, which lowers financial risk.

Things To Check

  • RC is only for people who already know how to code and want to spend most of their time programming.
  • It is a full-time commitment. RC says batches meet Monday through Friday, 11 am to 5 pm ET.
  • It is not a startup program, not a finance program, and not a career shortcut.
  • If you mainly want status or fast money rather than real programming immersion, this is the wrong fit.
