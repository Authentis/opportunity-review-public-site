Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is short enough to be a survivable experiment.
  • It creates repeated peer contact without requiring a long commitment.
  • It gives you a short way to experience a more international, purposeful group.
  • It is low-cost compared with premium gap-year travel.
  • It could fit side themes if the exchange topic is digital, environment, mobility, entrepreneurship, photography, or civic action.

Things To Check

  • It is not a single programme with one central application button.
  • Quality and peer fit will vary strongly by exchange topic and organiser.
  • It may feel too non-formal unless framed as an experiment in environment, not as a career solution.
  • It does not directly solve finance, startup, or academic direction.
