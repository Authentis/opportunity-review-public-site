Why It May Be Interesting

  • It offers a more ambitious and international academic environment than many nearby defaults.
  • Barcelona is a much more alive setting than several other academic options on the map.
  • It keeps business, entrepreneurship, and AI visibly connected.
  • The program’s internships, exchange semester, and hackathon-style learning make it less passive than a classic lecture-heavy route.

Things To Check

  • It is expensive.
  • It is still more business-led than math-led.
  • It could attract you for image and city more than for the day-to-day work.
  • Esade’s admissions process is selective and not casual.
