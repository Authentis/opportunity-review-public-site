Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is concrete and short: 25 May to 9 June 2026.
  • It is low-cost compared with premium reset programmes.
  • It has visible output and shared responsibility.
  • English is listed as the camp language.
  • The no-Wi-Fi / mountain setting creates a real break from the current social and digital context.

Things To Check

  • Physical discomfort and basic conditions are central, not incidental.
  • It may feel too rustic unless people around you frames it as a bounded experiment.
  • It does not advance fintech, startup, academic, or sailing identity directly.
  • It requires readiness to accept ordinary manual work.
