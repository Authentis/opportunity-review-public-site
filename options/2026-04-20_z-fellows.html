Why It May Be Interesting

  • It is one of the cleaner startup-preparation additions because it is shorter and lighter than Antler or EF-style founder routes.
  • It offers founder and VC-adjacent exposure without requiring you to drop out, quit a job, or already have a company.
  • It keeps the startup/venture branch alive without turning it into a fantasy of immediate prestige.

Things To Check

  • It is still best suited to people who are already building something or trying seriously to.
  • Travel to San Francisco or New York is a real practical hurdle if accepted.
  • The peer environment may be very startup-intense and could be status-heavy in practice.
