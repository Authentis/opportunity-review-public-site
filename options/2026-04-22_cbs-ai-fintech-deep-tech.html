Why It May Be Interesting

  • It connects finance, AI, fintech, blockchain / digital assets, risk, marketing, operations, and AI-first business strategy.
  • The prerequisite bar is clearer and lower than many graduate summer schools.
  • The course includes continuous feedback, quizzes, problem-solving, and a final exam.
  • Copenhagen is logistically plausible from Europe.
  • It tests whether academic structure feels better when content is closer to fintech / AI.

Things To Check

  • It is still a university course and may not solve isolation by itself.
  • Housing and daily structure outside class need checking.
  • It is subject-fit strong but not necessarily peer-container strong.
  • It requires willingness to study seriously for three weeks.
