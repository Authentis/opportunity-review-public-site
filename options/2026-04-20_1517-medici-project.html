Why It May Be Interesting

  • The philosophy is unusually compatible with you if you distrust standard educational status signals.
  • It values ambition, curiosity, and project-building over credentials.
  • It offers both belief capital and access to peers, mentors, and future support.

Things To Check

  • 1517 says direct grants are limited to teams based in North America because of regulatory constraints.
  • They still welcome people outside North America to write in, but support may be limited.
  • This is not a structured daily environment by itself.
  • It works best if you already have a concrete project you can explain credibly.
