Why It May Be Interesting

  • It squarely targets ambitious founders under 26.
  • The attraction is not just prestige; it offers peers, local chapters, mentoring, and investor access.
  • It could give the kind of international founder network you seems to imagine when you talks about better environments.
  • The summit founder route may provide a narrower first point of contact even before fellowship access.

Things To Check

  • Fellowship entry is nomination-based, so it is not immediately actionable like an open application.
  • It is clearly built for people already building something meaningful, not people still searching for direction.
  • The community is founder-centric rather than coding-, finance-, or confidence-first.
  • If you has no traction yet, this will likely feel distant and discouraging rather than helpful.
